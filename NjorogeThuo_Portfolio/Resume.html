Profile

Hello! I'm Samuel Thuo, an architectural designer and researcher passionate about creating multisensory spaces that enhance neuroinclusion, mental health, and wellbeing. My work focuses on designing environments that engage all the senses, challenging the modern trend of "nonsense places" that prioritize sight over other sensory experiences. I believe in the power of architecture to foster human connection and improve quality of life through thoughtful, inclusive design.
